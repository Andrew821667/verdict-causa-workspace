# Roadmap

## Repository Foundation

- Create public OSS repository.
- Add README, license, docs, Python skeleton, tests, CI, examples, and first issues.
- Make project status explicit: architectural/prototype stage only.

## Phase 0 - Universal core plus first institutional package

Goal: prove that the universal core works by loading the first full institutional package and running representative contractual relations tasks through the architecture.

Required work:

- universal Neuro-Symbolic Bootstrap Pipeline;
- Translation Layer with executive, professional, and forensic representations;
- governance infrastructure with candidate typology, sandbox, cross-review, and expiration;
- four-layer Knowledge Plane;
- Management Plane with cost budgets, SLA/depth modes, risk tiers, policy versions, and rollback classes;
- failure taxonomy and basic operational analytics;
- first contractual institutional package with vocabulary, authority model, temporal model, schema, mapping rules, contradiction taxonomy, legal operators, benchmark suite, red-team library, confidence policies, and activation policies.

## Phase 1 - Operational contour v1+ with Management Plane

Build case graph, active retrieval, multi-agent reasoning, provenance, source grounding, and versioned knowledge/policies. Start with `draft` and `standard` modes and T1-T3 risk tiers.

## Phase 2 - Expert resonance

Capture structured reviewer interactions and extract candidate calibration rules, gap heuristics, and argument enrichments. Nothing activates automatically.

## Phase 3 - Contradiction engine

Detect typed conflicts, propose candidate resolution patterns, and route them through governance with Red Team and Pre-Activation Sandbox.

## Phase 4 - Audit and replay layer plus Translation Layer

Provide reproducible decision traces, provenance graph, semantic diffs, rollback classes, and dual-tested Translation Layer.

## Phase 5 - Bounded counterfactual engine

Expand the Phase 0 one-step typed legal-operator library into MCTS-style exploration under compute budget and reusable micro-scenario libraries. Extend modes to `deep` and `research`, and risk tiers to T4-T6.

## Phase 6 - Reasoning profiles and enterprise readiness

Add fixed reasoning profiles, Formal Data Minimization Spec, and architecture preparation for tenant safety.

## Phase 7 - Multi-tenant plus customer-specific doctrine

Add real tenant isolation and customer-specific doctrine policy when a second corporate tenant makes it necessary.

## Phase 8 - Research branch: population diversification

Long-horizon controlled research with multiple reasoning instances. Not part of year-one production core.

## What not to build in year one

- Full population architecture.
- Cross-tenant pattern exchange.
- Broad coverage across many legal institutes.
- Aggressive autonomous evolution without double review.
- Unbounded counterfactual exploration.
- Per-user custom reasoning profiles.
- Autogenerated Translation Layer templates.
